## ðŸš¨ GAME MECHANICS

### Timer System
- Each mission has a time limit (3-6 minutes)
- Timer turns yellow at 60 seconds
- Timer turns red and pulses at 30 seconds
- Timer expires = MISSION FAILED

**Time Management:**
- Hacker doubles add +10 seconds
- Quick completions earn bonus crew rep
- Don't rush - precision over speed!

---

### Alert Level System
- Starts at 0%, increases with mistakes
- Different missions have different alert penalties
- Reaches 100% = MISSION FAILED
- Visual warnings at 70%+ (critical state)

**Alert Penalties by Mission:**
- Vault: +10% per wrong number
- Databreach: +15% per even number
- Diamond: +5% per segment below 15
- Casino: +8% per wrong pattern
- Big Score: Varies by phase

**Alert Management:**
- Stay calm when alert is high
- Focus on accuracy, not speed
- Low alert at mission end = bonus rep

---

### Player Rotation
- Players take turns throwing
- Rotation is automatic after each dart
- All 3 players work toward the same goal
- Communicate before throwing!

**Rotation Order:**
1. Hacker throws
2. Infiltrator throws
3. Demolitions throws
4. Back to Hacker...

- Individual player scores

- Higher scores earn more crew rep
- Role bonuses multiply points

**Point Values:**
- Base: 100 points per successful hit

- Role bonuses: Varies by mission
